A lover of abstract strategy games (Go, Shogi, chess), I created Keres to recapture what fascinates me: simple rules, infinite depth. As a perfectionist developer and maker, I designed every aspect of the project — from game engine to web platform, from engraved pieces to interface design.

My ambition: build a community around Keres, organize tournaments, and deliver a premium experience that honors the classics while opening new territory. Keres is designed to be played online first, because that's where we actually find time to play.
