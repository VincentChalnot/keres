A two-player duel, no randomness, no hidden information. Keres gives you a tactical space where stacking your pieces amplifies their movement, while separating them multiplies your threats. No fixed meta, no optimal build. Just you, your opponent, and the satisfaction of seeing your strategies come to life on the board.

- Zero randomness, complete information
- Your style, your strategies
- Games last 30 to 50 minutes
